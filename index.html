Welcome to My 2018 Baseball App

This app is Apple and Android friendly.



To download on IOS:
1. Open website in Safari.
2. Tap the box with the arrow pointing up.
3. Scroll the bottom row of icons until you find "Add to Home Screen".
4. Keep name or name it as you like.
5. Select Add on the top right corner.
6. Enjoy!



To download on Android:
1. Open website in Chrome.
2. tap the 3 bar icon.
3. Select add to Home Screen.
4. Enjoy!
